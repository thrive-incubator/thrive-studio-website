§01Why now

The workforce nearly doubled. Outcomes didn't move.

Pediatric mental health may be this generation's defining crisis, and the field keeps losing it — because it treats the problem as individual and clinical when the leverage is workforce and environmental.

More clinicians won't close the gap in a generation. The children who need the most help spend almost none of their hours with a clinician. They spend them with the educators, home visitors, and caregivers already in the room. That's where every venture we build lives.

§02What we build

We build through the system, not against it.

01 / LEVERAGE

Workforce, not clinicians

The leverage is the people already in the room. Equip the workforce around the child and you reach an order of magnitude more children than any clinical model.

02 / MATH

$3–15M ARR, profitable

Sustainable companies built lean on AI, not moonshots. ~80% survival across the portfolio, modest multiples, capital that recycles into the next venture.

03 / ORIGIN

Validated before it exists

Ideas come from clinicians and researchers and are developed inside the Studio. We do the research, discovery, and prototyping — then pair the right operator to a validated idea.

§03Proof it's real

Attunify.

Our first independent spin-out, launched after a roughly five-month incubation. The founding cohort opened in June 2026 — defining the national competency standard for Infant & Early Childhood Mental Health Consultation, the missing tier between teacher training and clinical care.
