Why now

The workforce nearly doubled. Outcomes didn't move.

Pediatric mental health may be this generation's defining crisis, and the field keeps losing it — because it treats the problem as individual and clinical when the leverage is workforce and environmental.

More clinicians won't close the gap in a generation. The children who need the most help spend almost none of their hours with a clinician. They spend them with the early-childhood educators, home visitors, and caregivers already in the room. That's where every venture we build lives.

What we build

We build through the system, not against it.

01

Workforce, not clinicians

The leverage is the people already in the room. Equip the workforce around the child and you reach an order of magnitude more children than any clinical model.

02

$3–15M ARR, profitable

Sustainable companies built lean on AI, not moonshots. Roughly 80% survival across the portfolio, modest multiples, capital that recycles into the next venture.

03

Validated before it exists

Ideas are brought by clinicians and researchers and developed inside the Studio. We do the desk research, discovery, and prototyping — then pair the right operator to a validated idea.

The model

Three outcomes. No default.

Every venture moves through four gated stages toward one of three honest outcomes:

Spin out  as an independent company Spin in  as a durable Center program Shut down  with a clean post-mortem
