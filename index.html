Not another venture studio

We don't chase ideas. We build on what the field already trusts.

Most studios talk about speed and validation. We start from something the field already trusts — a proven model, an evidence base, a recognized name — whether it comes from Georgetown, a partner institution, or your own practice. Then we put the weight of Georgetown behind it.

And we're not neutral about what we build. The biggest gains for children and families come from strengthening the people already in the room — educators, home visitors, caregivers — not more clinicians, and not another app.

Why it works

An instant brand, and doors that open.

The Thrive Center is the SAMHSA-designated National Center of Excellence for early-childhood mental-health consultation, with thirty years of research, training, and relationships behind it.

That credibility doesn't have to be earned from scratch — it transfers to every company we build, opening doors to the systems, customers, and payers that decide whether a company lives.
