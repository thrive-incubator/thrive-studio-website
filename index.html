Why it works

Credibility that transfers.

Georgetown’s Thrive Center built the models the field runs on, across children’s mental health, disabilities, and early childhood. Its work has been funded and recognized across the federal government, from Health and Human Services to the Departments of Defense and Education. It developed the national model for early childhood mental health consultation, and it is the federally designated University Center for Excellence in Developmental Disabilities (UCEDD) for the District of Columbia.

None of that trust and credibility has to be earned from scratch. It transfers to every company we build, and it opens doors other startups work years to access.
